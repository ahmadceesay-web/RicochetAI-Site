FAQ

Common Questions

What types of clinics do you work with?
We work with a wide range of UK healthcare and wellness clinics — physiotherapy, osteopathy, chiropractic, aesthetics, dental, GP surgeries, mental health practices, personal training studios, and more. If you have patients and admin, we can help.
Do you integrate with booking systems like Cliniko or Jane App?
Yes. We integrate with most clinic management software including Cliniko, Jane App, Nookal, Acuity, Fresha, Pabau, and others. We'll confirm compatibility during your free discovery call.
Is patient data handled securely and GDPR compliantly?
Absolutely. All automations are built with UK GDPR compliance in mind. Patient data is handled according to ICO guidelines, and we'll work within your existing data processing agreements.
How long does it take to go live?
Most clinic automation projects are live within 1–3 weeks. A straightforward appointment reminder system can be running in as little as 3–5 days. You'll get a clear timeline after the audit.
Do we need to change our existing software?
No. We build around what you already use. There's no need to switch platforms or retrain your team on new software — we slot seamlessly into your existing setup.
